KAZAKH TRAVEL

Kazakhstan, a land of vast steppe and soaring mountains, beckons intrepid travelers with its untamed beauty and rich heritage.
Discover its hidden wonders and be inspired by the endless possibilities.

City

1. Almaty:
Almaty is the largest city and tourist center of Kazakhstan.
It is located in a picturesque natural environment with mountains and lakes.
Here you can ski in winter and enjoy mountain hikes in summer.
The city is also rich in cultural attractions and restaurants offering a wealth of national and world cuisine.

2. Astana:
The capital of Kazakhstan, Astana (now Nur-Sultan), is a modern and ambitious city
with impressive architecture and many new construction projects. Here you can see the Bayterek Tower
and numerous modern bridges and buildings. The city also serves as an important hub for business events.

3.Aktobe: Aktobe is a city with a diverse culture and rich history.
It is known for its parks, mosques, and architectural landmarks
like the Ali Beyret Monument and the Sharishkul Mosque. The city
offers a blend of modern and historical attractions for tourists to explore.

4. Aktau:
Aktau is a port city on the shores of the Caspian Sea.
Here you can enjoy the beaches, as well as explore the coastal areas and archaeological sites.
The city is also known for its modern architecture and seafood restaurants.

5. Atyrau:
Atyrau is another important port city located on the shores of the Caspian Sea.
This city is known for its oil and gas industries.
Tourists can see modern industrial complexes and visit the Caspian coast.

6. Karagandy:
Karaganda is a city with a rich history of the coal industry.
Important tourist sites are the Museum of the Karaganda Region and
the Memorial Complex "Shakhterskoe to the Victims".

7. Oral:
Oral, also known as Uralsk, is situated near the Chagan Lake, offering stunning
natural beauty. Tourists can visit the Dostoevsky Museum to explore the influence of classical
literature on Kazakh culture. The city is also home to significant religious landmarks like the
Central Mosque, an important religious site and architectural attraction.

8. Kostanay:
Kostanay is a city with a central square that serves as the heart of the city.
Tourists can admire the monument to Mickiewicz and the Regional Administration building.
The city is also home to Gorky Park, one of the oldest parks in Kazakhstan with amusements and attractions.
